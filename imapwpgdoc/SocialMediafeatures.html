Social Media features:


The location for social media features such as Twitter and Instagam have been randomize to protect the privacy of the posters, and it should be taken as approximate only.


Twitter:

This feature can only be refreshed (getting new data) once every 15 minutes. To refresh this feature, remove it from the map by de-selecting it, then re-selecting it. This feature will retrieve tweets that covers the whole city.


Instagram:

This feature can only be refreshed (getting new data) once every 15 minutes. To refresh this feature, remove it from the map by de-selecting it, then re-selecting it. Due to the limitation imposed by Instagram, this feature can only retrieve posts within 5KM radius base on the center of the map. If you are interested in a particular area (eg, downtown), move the center of the map to that area first before selected this feature.
