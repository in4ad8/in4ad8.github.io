About:


Welcome and thanks for downloading ImapWPG App. I hope you'll like and find this App useful.


This App was created to provide a fast and an efficient way of finding information about spatially mapped data based on your current location.


The current data set include City of Winnipeg recreation facilities, City of Winnipeg boundaries, health care facilities, bus stops,social media and much more... .


This App will remain 100% free, no charge for downloading, no In-App Purchases, no Advertisements as long as the following conditions holds true

-Google map android API does not charge for using their service.

-Any of the data source providers does not charge for using their data.
